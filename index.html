About

Functionalities

This is a project for controlling your computer with your hand geastures. There is two modes in project whichs are Mouse mode and Watching mode. Mouse mode cantains mouse movement and clip board actions and Watching mode contains Video control.

Mouse

Mouse Movement, Right/Left/Left Dobule Click, Scroll Up/Down

Clip Board

Copy, Cut, Paste

Video

Fast Forward, Rewind, Pause/Play, Volume Settings

Mouse

You can use Mouse functions with specific hand gestures.
Here are the functions and hand gestures.

Mouse movement

while only your index finger is open, you can control the mouse movement acording to where your index finger's top is

Left Click

While your index and middle are both open and all the other fingers are closed, you can use the left click funtion with touching your middle and index figers's tops to each other

Double Left Click

While your pinky and ring finger is closed and the others are open, you can use the double left click funtion with touching your middle and index figers's tops to each other

Right Click

While your thumb and ring finger is closed and the others are open, you can use the right click funtion with touching your middle and index figers's tops to each other

Scroll Up

While only your pinky is open, you can use the Scroll Up funtion.

Scroll Down

While only your thumb is open, you can use the Scroll Down funtion.

Clip Board Actions

You can use Clip board actions with specified hand gestures
whichs are copy, cut and paste.

Copy

While your index finger and thumb is open, you can use the copy funtion.

Cut

While your ring finger and pinky is open, you can use the cut funtion.

Paste

While your thumb is close and all the others are open, you can use the paste funtion.

Video Control

You can also use simple video interactions with hand gestures.
Funtions and hand gestures to use them are mantioned below.

Volume control

While your index and middle are both open and all the other fingers are closed, you can use the Volume settings funtions with touching your middle and index figers's tops to each other and moving your hand up or down.

Fast Forward

While only your pinky is open, you can use the Fasat Forward funtion.

Rewind

While only your thumb is open, you can use the Rewind funtion.

Start/Stop

While your all fingers are open, you can use the Start/Stop funtion.
